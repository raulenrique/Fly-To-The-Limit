Let's Go.

Our flights.

We offer a range of flight packages. From gliding over the lake to doing air acrobatics in our stunt plane, we cater to anyone and everyone looking to experience the spectacular views.

If your an avid or keen tramper we also offer a pick up and drop off service around the area so you can explore of the many tramping routes in the area.

Get to know us.

We’re a small independent business, passionate about sharing the beautiful South Island in a new and spectacular way through our scenic flights.

We have been flying for over 20 years which has given us the time to find the most beautiful scenic views plus we have the reputation to back it up.

All our staff are highly experienced and own the craft that they fly. Please take your time getting to know our staff so you are assured that you are in professional and safe hands.

The Amphibian

An amphibious aircraft or amphibian is an aircraft that can take off and land on both land and water. Fixed-wing amphibious aircraft are seaplanes (flying boats and floatplanes) that are equipped with retractable wheels, at the expense of extra weight and complexity, plus diminished range and fuel economy compared to planes designed for land or water only.
